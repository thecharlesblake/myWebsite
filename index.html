Who am I?

Good question! Well, I'm a Computer Science student at St Andrews University, going into my third year in September (on a four year undergrad course). I wanted to make this website as a demonstration of some of my skills, and really just to practice some web development.

Also, to advertise to recruiters, HR people, and basically anyone who might be able to give me an internship/job, that I'm the kind of person they might want on board! If you fit that description, why not check out my digital CV?

Responsive Design

This website has been built using Twitter's Bootstrap framework, which means it's mobile-responsive. That means whether you choose to browse on desktop, tablet or mobile (on any browser) the web page should adjust itself intelligently to fit the size of your display. Feel free to try it out!
